Plan'it Teknoy is Different

This application's end goal is to implement student planner with a dynamic school calendar which can be edited and updated by selected personnel for the university's constituents

About Us

The developers of Plan'it Teknoy are students from Cebu Institute of Technology - University. The developers are graduating students from the Information Technology department. They aim to resolve the pain points experienced by students by creating Plan'it Teknoy.

FEATURES

Plan'it Teknoy allows students to manage their schedule by using the calendar planner feature of the application. It does not end there since in that calendar, a dynamic calendar is embedded filled with the university's events.

Announcements and News

By using Plan'it Teknoy's Announcements and News feature, the students do not need to transfer to other platforms to be notified, they can access all the announcements and news from all the platforms the university has posted the notification.

School Calendar

The application is being pre loaded with the University's event list for the entire school year. What's great about this is that it is embedded in the calendar planner of the students.
